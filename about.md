## We build AI that actually ships

Most companies don't have an AI problem. They have a *deployment* problem. The models exist. The APIs exist. What's missing is the engineering to turn an impressive demo into a reliable system that runs in production, handles edge cases, and delivers measurable business value.

That's what we do. Cartesian Trees is an AI solution partner — we work alongside your team to design, build, and deploy AI-powered systems that solve real problems. Not proof-of-concepts that live in notebooks. Working software.

## What we bring to the table

### LLM Integration & Agentic Systems

The shift from static software to AI-native applications is happening now. We help companies navigate it:

- **LLM-powered applications** — integrating models from OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, and open-source alternatives into your product stack with proper prompt engineering, guardrails, and evaluation pipelines
- **Agentic architectures** — designing multi-step AI agents that can reason, use tools, and execute complex workflows autonomously
- **RAG systems** — retrieval-augmented generation pipelines that ground LLM responses in your proprietary data, with vector databases, embedding strategies, and chunking optimized for your domain
- **Fine-tuning & evaluation** — when off-the-shelf models aren't enough, we fine-tune on your data and build evaluation frameworks so you know exactly how well your system performs

### Bayesian Intelligence & Probabilistic Systems

This is where our roots are. We hold **Jeune Entreprise Innovante (JEI)** status from the French government — recognition reserved for companies doing genuine R&D innovation.

Our [open-source research](https://github.com/cartesiantrees/postgres-bayes) brings Bayesian inference directly into PostgreSQL: MCMC sampling, Gibbs sampling, variational inference, and approximate Bayesian computation — all running where your data lives. No more shuttling data between databases and external ML pipelines.

We apply this to real-world problems: recommendation engines, anomaly detection, market regime detection, and any scenario where decisions must be made under uncertainty.

### Production AI Engineering

Getting a model to work is the easy part. Getting it to work *reliably, at scale, in production* is where most projects fail. We handle the full stack:

- **API development** — Python, Django, FastAPI backends that serve AI models with proper authentication, rate limiting, and monitoring
- **Infrastructure** — GCP, Kubernetes deployments with CI/CD pipelines, model versioning, and automated rollbacks
- **Data pipelines** — from raw data to clean, structured inputs your models can actually use
- **Frontend** — React & Next.js interfaces that make AI capabilities accessible to your end users

## How we work

We don't do waterfall proposals and 6-month timelines. Our approach is designed for the pace AI moves at:

**1. Understand the problem** — We start by understanding what business outcome you're trying to achieve. Not what model you want to use, but what decision you're trying to make better.

**2. Prototype fast** — Within the first 1-2 weeks, we build a working prototype that validates whether the approach is viable. If it's not, we find out early and adjust.

**3. Harden for production** — Once the approach is validated, we engineer it for reliability: error handling, edge cases, monitoring, fallbacks, and proper testing.

**4. Transfer knowledge** — We work embedded with your team. When we leave, your team owns the system and understands how to maintain and extend it.
